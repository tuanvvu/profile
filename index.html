Tuan Vu

Hi, I am Tuan, a senior air quality scientist at Imperial College London, UK. My research has focused on better understanding air pollution and its impacts on human health & climate change. I am also interested in numerical analysis & applied statistics/machine learning for source apportionment of PM and air pollution modelling.
